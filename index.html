About

GRAPH!C is a multi-disciplined rapper from Toronto specializing in lyricism. He started songwriting at the age of 13. He acquired a taste for music by absorbing the influence of the artists he heard as a child. taking the time to learn the language and practice recording techniques taught to him by industry professionals sets GRAPH!C apart from many of the hip-hop acts that listeners experience. Putting his own spin on the usual master, he has gained a taste for his own sound by creating a unique flare in his music.
